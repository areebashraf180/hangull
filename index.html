Our Story

A journey woven through generations of Kashmiri craftsmanship

Hangul is more than a brand — it is a bridge between the ancient looms of Kashmir and the modern world. Our name, inspired by the majestic Hangul deer of Kashmir, symbolises grace, rarity, and the untamed beauty of our homeland.

For generations, our family has been immersed in the art of pashmina making. From the high-altitude plateaus where Changthangi goats graze, to the skilled hands that spin, weave, and embroider each shawl, we oversee every step with devotion to quality.

Every Hangul pashmina is handcrafted — never machine-made. Our artisans spend weeks and sometimes months on a single piece. This is not mass production. This is living art.

We founded Hangul with a simple mission: to bring authentic Kashmiri pashmina to discerning customers worldwide, while ensuring fair compensation for the artisans who make it all possible.

Pure & Authentic

100% genuine Kashmiri pashmina, sourced ethically from Changthangi goats at 14,000 feet altitude.

Handcrafted With Time

Each pashmina takes weeks to months of meticulous handwork. We never rush artistry.

Fair & Sustainable

We work directly with artisan families, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices.
