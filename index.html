Our Story

A journey woven through generations

Hangul is more than a brand — it is a bridge between the ancient looms of Kashmir and the modern world. Our name, inspired by the majestic Hangul deer of Kashmir, symbolises grace, rarity, and untamed beauty.

For generations, our family has been immersed in pashmina making. From the high-altitude plateaus where Changthangi goats graze, to the skilled hands that spin, weave, and embroider each shawl, we oversee every step.

Every Hangul pashmina is handcrafted — never machine-made. Our artisans spend weeks and sometimes months on a single piece. This is not mass production. This is living art.

We founded Hangul with a simple mission: to bring authentic Kashmiri pashmina to discerning customers worldwide, while ensuring fair compensation for the artisans.

Pure & Authentic

100% genuine Kashmiri pashmina, sourced ethically from Changthangi goats at 14,000 feet. GI Tag certified.

Handcrafted With Time

Each pashmina takes weeks to months of meticulous handwork. We never rush artistry.

Fair & Sustainable

We work directly with artisan families, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices.
